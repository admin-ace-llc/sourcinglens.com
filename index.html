      How SourcingLens works

      1. Normalize your current lane

      Your current supplier country and unit cost act as the anchor. If you don’t provide an HS code, we call GPT‑4.1‑mini with your product description to infer a likely HS6 family.

      2. Apply country profiles

      For each profiled country, we apply internal multipliers to estimate base manufacturing cost, tariffs, and freight per unit. These are heuristics, not official rates.

      3. Compare & export

      We rank countries by directional landed cost, highlight potential savings, and let you download the full table as CSV to share with your team.

      Always validate HS codes, duties, and regulations with your customs broker or trade counsel before acting. SourcingLens is an analysis aid, not a filing or compliance system.

      FAQ

      Is this a customs or legal tool?

      No. SourcingLens is an analysis aid only. It helps you get directional clarity before you spend time and money on formal quotes or broker work.

      How accurate are HS suggestions?

      GPT‑4.1‑mini is very strong at pattern recognition, but HS classification is nuanced. Always confirm with your broker. Treat HS suggestions here as a starting point, not a final answer.

      Will you store my product details?

      This MVP is designed to be light touch. Avoid entering sensitive information. If you self-host, your own configuration controls what is logged.
