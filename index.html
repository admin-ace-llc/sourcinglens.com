    How SourcingLens works

    1. Free 1-vs-1 comparison

    The free tool lets you sanity-check one alternative lane against your current supplier using simplified cost and duty heuristics. No HS codes, no CSV ‚Äì just a quick directional read.

    2. Starter & Pro (AI-enabled)

    Starter and Pro unlock GPT-4.1-mini-based HS suggestions and 5-country comparisons. You get CSV exports, narrative summaries, compliance notes, and for Pro, a richer multi-SKU report with a risk panel.

    3. Pro Monthly & beyond

    Pro Monthly adds a light account layer backed by Supabase so you can store and revisit analyses. Business and Enterprise tiers (coming soon) will add higher SKU volumes, team-oriented dashboards, and API options.

    SourcingLens is an analysis aid only. All HS suggestions and landed-cost outputs are indicative and must be validated with your customs broker, legal counsel, or other qualified advisors before you act.

    FAQ

    Is the free tool "enough"?

    The free 1-vs-1 view is excellent for a quick sniff test. If you're seriously considering a lane change, Starter or Pro give you a more complete picture across five countries with HS-aware assumptions.

    How accurate are HS suggestions?

    GPT-4.1-mini is strong at pattern-matching, but HS classification is nuanced. Treat suggestions as a starting point only and always confirm with your broker or trade counsel.

    How do accounts work for Pro Monthly?

    Pro Monthly uses Supabase for email + magic link sign-in and a simple analyses table to store your runs. You can sign in from any browser where your Supabase project is configured.
